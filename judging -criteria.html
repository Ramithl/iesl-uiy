Judging Criteria

  1. Explaination of the problem and people affected
  2. Expressing the idea clearly with its positive impact
  3. Comparing of the idea with what already exists (if applicable) and/or Highlighted the uniqueness of the solution
  4. Demonstrate the idea in tangible form (prototype or as a diagram)
  5. Commercial viability of the idea
